Better Half

Ghana / 2023
Educational Center / Competition

What if we could redefine our approach to project completion, offering an early start with half of the main structure and programs, rather than waiting for a prolonged period to utilize the entire space?


In the context of Ghana's hot and humid climate, "Better Half" optimizes a limited budget by strategically cutting programs in half. The primary focus is on constructing the main frame and solid walls for half of all spaces, while the remaining areas can be completed with the active involvement of students and residents, fostering a sense of ownership and engagement.
