Mohammad Fasahat is an architectural designer and technologist based in Charlotte, NC. His work bridges the gap between technical documentation and high-fidelity narrative visualization.

Currently at Little Diversified Architectural Consulting, he works within the Mixed-Use Studio, leading R&D initiatives to integrate AI workflows into traditional design processes. He holds a Master of Architecture from UNCC, where he served as a research assistant at the Integrated Design Research Lab.
